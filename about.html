About Danisha Lowe

The Host With The Most

Born in Paterson, New Jersey, and now proudly rooted in Eastern North Carolina, Danisha Lowe brings big-city confidence with Southern charm to every event she hosts.

Known as “The Host With The Most,” Danisha is a dynamic public speaker with a commanding yet warm presence. Her voice carries confidence, clarity, and charisma, effortlessly guiding audiences while keeping energy high and guests fully engaged. Whether she is leading a wedding reception, hosting a milestone celebration, or coordinating a formal event, she blends professionalism with personality to create unforgettable experiences.

Danisha’s hosting style is polished, organized, and interactive. She naturally reads the room, adapts to any crowd, and supports all vibes from elegant and upscale to fun and high-energy. Her goal is always the same: to create a seamless flow while ensuring her clients feel completely stress-free.

As a Day-Of Wedding Coordinator and Emcee, she takes pride in managing timelines, vendor communication, and event transitions so her clients can fully enjoy their special moments. Her attention to detail, leadership presence, and calming demeanor allow her to handle behind-the-scenes logistics while delivering an engaging, memorable event in front of the mic.

In partnership with DJ SmooveK, Danisha also co-leads “A Whole Vibe Entertainment,” offering clients the complete experience—professional hosting and high-quality DJ services all in one. Together, they bring energy, structure, and seamless coordination to events, allowing clients to book both a Host and DJ for a fully unified, stress-free celebration. From curated playlists to crowd engagement and smooth transitions, they don’t just throw parties—they create an entire vibe.

Danisha doesn’t just host events, she creates organized, elevated experiences filled with energy, excellence, and lasting memories.
